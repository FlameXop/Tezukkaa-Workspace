Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

At Tezuka Planner, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the essential information needed to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Your email address and name (used for login and identification).
  • Usage Data: Tasks, project names, and workflow status (stored to sync across your devices).
  • Payment Info: Processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide the task management syncing service.
  • Verify your subscription status (Free Trial vs Premium).
  • Send critical account updates (e.g., password resets).

3. Data Security

Your data is stored in secure cloud databases (Firebase) with industry-standard encryption. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at Tezukkaa@gmail.com.
